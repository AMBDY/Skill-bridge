About SkillBridge

Bridging African Talent with Global Opportunity

SkillBridge is a premium marketplace platform combining freelance services, e-commerce, and a job board — built specifically for Africa, starting in Nigeria, with a vision for global expansion.

🛡️ Trust First

Multi-level KYC verification and escrow-protected payments on every transaction.

🌍 Borderless

Real-time multilingual chat with AI translation across 16 languages including Yoruba, Hausa, and Igbo.

🤖 AI-Powered

Smart search ranking, recommendations, fraud detection, and price suggestions.

Our Mission

To empower every African creator, freelancer, and business with a trustworthy platform to showcase their work, connect with clients worldwide, and get paid securely — starting from Nigeria and expanding globally.

We believe African talent deserves a world-stage platform that respects local context, supports local payment methods, and protects every party in every transaction.
